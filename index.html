Skateboard in London

London has had a storied history in skateboarding culture. It’s been decades since skateboarding has entered the mainstream, but with London’s skateparks being saved and the rapid growth of brands like Supreme and Palace, it’s suddenly cooler than ever.

This map shows 6 popular skateparks and 4 top rated skate shops in London. Click the helmet marks to browse park details, photos, locations and further information, while the skateboard buttons mark the 4 popular skate shops.

Drag Pegman onto the skatepark positions to open Street View. Click anywhere in the map to close the infowindow.
